THE BASICS

My name is Brad, and I really believe in design. Not just graphic design, or web design, or UI and UX. I believe in design as a force. For me, there’s no better moment than when something comes together conceptually, functionally, and beautifully. I want to help people make those moments every day.

So, a bit about me. I’ll say up front that I am a bit of a graphics nerd. I like margins and glyphs and colors; don’t even get me started on typographic hierarchy. But, (and yes, I started a sentence with a conjunction but believe it or not that’s okay) I also love functionality. I don’t care how lovely a flyer is if I can’t gleam information from it. I won’t use a gorgeous minimalist website if I can’t follow the login process.


MORE RECENTLY

I love learning and getting better at what I do. Within the past year I have relocated across the country. Rather than jump back into the same work I had been doing, I took some time off to improve in areas where I felt I needed more exposure.

I wanted to learn more about digital design and UI and UX principles, so I took some online classes and feel like it’s the kind I work I was meant to do. It combines what I love about graphic design with the satisfaction of learning if what I made was useable.


THE GIST

The point of everything you just read is that I am ready to embark on my next cliché… I mean journey. I want to use my new skills to make beautiful work. I want to design with functionality in mind and will jump at the chance to make prototypes. If you are in the market for a designer who is focused on getting results, I’d love to hear from you.

I could keep going, but all the guidelines on the web say not to bore your site visitors with walls of text, so I’ll wrap it up with this: I am available, and I'm looking for more opportunities to flex my mental muscles and help make great things.
