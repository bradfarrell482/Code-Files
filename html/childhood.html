Just Stating The Obvious

This is a quick concept of a PSA campaign focusing on children and tech use. Today we use computers and smart devices so regularly that it hardly occurs to some that children might be missing out on the best parts of being a kid. This campaign is meant to give kids and parents a gentle nudge to put down their devices for awhile and have fun other ways.
