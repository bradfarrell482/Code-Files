With tech companies and mobile developers cracking down on their emoticon images, Hill Holliday took the cautious route.

THE NEED FOR SOMETHING NEW

While I was interning at Hill Holiday in 2016, an interesting challenge was posed to the creative staff. Recent news had broken that companies were beginning to file lawsuits claiming misuse of emojis by other companies. As a precaution, the decision was made that Hill Holliday needed its own icon library for some upcoming ads.

I was asked to help create some of the “object” emojis such as sporting equipment and other odds and ends. This page has a sampling of some that made it through the approval process.

DUNKIN' DONUTS AD

Shortly after my internship ended, this ad began running on video sites like Youtube. Though I wasn’t responsible for the video, the ad does include the chocolate bar emoticon I supplied.
